FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this coaching for? −

Mid-career professionals, tech talent, and executives who want a faster, more strategic job search and stronger offers.

What happens on the free clarity call? +

We review your current situation, identify the biggest bottlenecks, and map your highest-leverage next steps so you leave with a clear plan.

How is this different from a recruiter? +

Recruiters work for employers. Dylan works for you on strategy, positioning, interview narrative, and salary negotiation.

Do you help with resume, LinkedIn, and interviews? +

Yes. Coaching includes targeted positioning, messaging updates, interview prep, and offer negotiation support.

How quickly do clients see results? +

Most clients gain clarity and momentum within the first two weeks, with stronger interview pipelines soon after execution starts.

Do you offer 1:1 and group coaching? +

Yes. Programs include both private coaching and group formats depending on your goals, timeline, and support preference.

Is this only for people in Austin? +

No. Dylan is based in Austin, TX and works with clients remotely across the U.S. and internationally.

How do I get started? +

Book a free clarity call, share your goals, and we’ll decide together on the fastest path and right level of support.
